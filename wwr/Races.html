Choosing a Race to Play

Once you roll for your abilit stats, you are then able to start picking out a race and class. now there are many combinations to choose from, but certain combinations are recommended over others. on this page, the kinds of races will be put in alphabetical order with a brief information that can be considered in portant (Ability score adjustments, size, and base movement speed). Underneath the table, is all the information of the races. most of the racial traits have names, but I took some creative liberty to create names for the ones that don't have a catchy name.

Dwarf

Dwarves are known for their resilience and strength. They are skilled craftsmen and miners. The racial traits a dwarf can have are:

  • Level Adjustments: Dwarves have a level adjustments of +2 CON, and -2 CHA.
  • Size: Dwarves are Medium creatures. while short, dwarves are still stocky individuals.
  • Movement Speed: Dwarves have a land speed of 20ft.
  • Darkvision: Dwarves can see up to 60 ft. (Black and white vision in darkness).
  • Stone Cunning: Dwarves gain a +2 racial bonus in identifying stone work, such as hidden doors, traps, construction, etc.
  • Weapon familiarity: War axes, and urgoshes are considered martial instead of exotic weaponry towards dwarves.
  • Stability: Dwarves have a +4 to ability checks against being trip, bull rushed, etc. (while staying on the ground).
  • Extreme Filtration: The Dwarven body allows it to be able to fight poisons more easily. Thus, gaining a +2 bonus to saving throws against poisons.
  • Magical Resistance: dwarves gain a +2 racial bonus against spells/spell like ability’s due to having a natural resistance against magic.
  • Natural Enemies: : Dwarves gain a +1 to attack rolls against Orcs and Goblinoids.
  • NO TOUCH GIANT: +4 dodge bonus to AC against Giant type monsters. “we dwarves are just too hard to see for them sky heads!”
  • Dwarven Eyes: gain a +2 bonus to the appraise check towards stone, metals, and gems.

Elf

Elves are graceful, intelligent, and deeply connected to nature and magic.

Half-Orc

Half-Orcs are powerful warriors, combining the strength of Orcs with the determination of humans.

Human

Humans are versatile and ambitious, excelling in various roles and adapting to any situation.

humans have no ability score adjustment, considered medium creatures, and have a base movement speed of 30ft.
