Choosing a Race to Play

Once you roll for your abilit stats, you are then able to start picking out a race and class. now there are many combinations to choose from, but certain combinations are recommended over others.

on this page, the kinds of races will be put in alphabetical order with a brief information that can be considered in portant (Ability score adjustments, size, and base movement speed).

Dwarf

Dwarves are known for their resilience and strength. They are skilled craftsmen and miners.

Elf

Elves are graceful, intelligent, and deeply connected to nature and magic.

Half-Orc

Half-Orcs are powerful warriors, combining the strength of Orcs with the determination of humans.

Human

Humans are versatile and ambitious, excelling in various roles and adapting to any situation.

humans have no ability score adjustment, considered medium creatures, and have a base movement speed of 30ft.
