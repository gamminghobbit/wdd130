Casters and their Spell Lists

This part of the website is meant to have all the spell caster classes' Spells in list form. each list will consist of the specific class, followed by the level of spell, and in alphabetical order. when it comes to classes that are similiar to thw Sorceror/Wizard, they will be seperated by level, then the School of magic (Envocation, Illusio, Necromancy, etc.) then by alphabetical. in time you can click on the spell name and you can then go to a page that will have the desired spell with a more detailed information needed to use the spell in a game of Dungeons and Dragons.

Bard Spells

    0-level spells(cantrips)

    • Dancing Lights: Creates torches or other lights.
    • Daze: Humanoid creature of 4 HD or less loses next action.
    • Detect Magic: Detects spells and magic items within 60ft.
    • Flare: Dazzles one creature(-1 onattack rolls)
    • Ghost Sound: Figment sounds.
    • Know Direction: You discern north.
    • Light: Object shines like a torch.
    • Lullaby: Makes subject drowsy; -5 on spot and listen checks, -2 on will saves against sleep.
    • Mage Hand: 5-pound telekinesis
    • Mending: Makes minor repairs on an object.

    1st-level spells

    • Alarm: Wards an area for 2 hours/level.
    • Animate Rope: Makes a rope move at your command.
    • Cause Fear: One creature of 5 HD or less flees for 1d4 rounds.
    • Charm Person: Makes one person your friend.
    • Comprehend Languages: You understand all spoken and written languages.
    • Cure Light Wounds: Cures 1d8 damage +1/level (max +5)
    • Detect Secret Doors: Reveals hidden doors within 60ft.
    • Disguise Self: Changes you appearance.
    • Erase: Mundane or Magical writing vanishes.
    • Expeditious Retreat: Your speed is increases by 30ft.
    • Feather Fall: Objects or creatures falls slowly.

    2nd-level spells

    • Alter Self: Assume form of a similar creature.
    • Animal Messenger: Sends a Tiny animal to a specifc place.
    • Animal Trance: Fascinates 2d6 HD of Animals.
    • Blindness/Deafness: Makes a subject blind of deaf.
    • Blur: Attacks miss subject 20% of the time.
    • Calm Emotions: Calms creatures, negating emotion effects.
    • Cat's Grace: Subject gains +4 to Dex for 1 min./level.
    • Cure Moderate Wounds: Cures 2d8 damage +1/level (max +10).
    • Darkness: 20ft radius of supernatural shadow.
    • Daze Monster: Living creature of 6 HD or less loses next action.

Cleric Spells

0-level Spells (cantrips)

  • Create Water: Creates 2 gallons/level of pure water.
  • Cure Minor Wounds: Cures 1 point of damage.
  • Detect Magic: Detects spells and magic items within 60ft.
  • Detect Posion: Detects poison in one creature or object.
  • Guidance: +1 on attack roll, saving throw, or skill check.
  • Inflict Minor Wounds: Touch attack, 1 point of damage.
  • Light: Object shines like a torch.
  • Mending: Makes minor repairs on an object.
  • Purify Food and Drink: Purifies 1 cu.ft./level of food and water.
  • Read Magic: Read scrolls and spellbooks.
  • Resistance: subject gains +1 on saving throws.
  • Virtue: Subject gains 1 temporary hp.

1st-level Spells.

  • Bane: Enemies take -1 on attack rolls and saves against fear.
  • Bless: Allies gain +1 on attack rolls and saves against fear.
  • Bless Water: Makes hloky water.
  • Cause Fear: One creature of 5 HD or less fleas for 1d4 rounds.
  • Command: One subject obeys selected command for 1 round.
  • Comprehend Languages: You understand all spoken and written languages.
  • Cure Light Wounds: Cures 1d8 damage +1/level (max +5)
  • Curse Water: Makes unholy water.
  • Death Watch: Reveals how near death subjects within 30ft are.
  • Detect Chaos/Evil/Good/Law: Reveals creatures, spells, or objects of selected alignment.
  • Detect Undead: Reveals undead within 60ft.
  • Divine Favor: You gain +1 per three levels on attack and damage rolls.
  • Doom: One subject takes -2 on attack rolls, damage rolls, saves, and checks.
  • Endure Elements: Exist comfortably in hot or cold enviroments.
  • Entropic Shield: Ranged attacks against you have 20% miss chance.
  • Hide from Undead: Undead can't perceive one subject/level.
  • Inflict Light Wounds: Touch deals 1d8 damage +1/level (max +5)
  • Magic Stone: Three stones gain +1 on attack, deal 1d6 +1 damage.
  • Magic Weapon: Weapon gains +1 bonus.
  • Obscuring Mist: Fog surrounds you.
  • Protection from Chaos/Evil/Good/Law: +2 to AC and saves, counter mind control, hedge out elementals and outsiders.
  • Remove Fear: Suppresses fear or gives +4 on saves against fear for one subject + one per four levels.
  • Sanctuary: Opponenets can't attack you, and you can't attack.
  • Shield of Faith: Aura grants +2 or higher deflection bonus.
  • Summon Monster 1: Calls extraplanar creature to fight for you.
